Garlic Mushrooms are highly nutritous and are easy to make with relativley cheap ingredients. Garlic Mushrooms are also extremley delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pounds sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • teaspoon dried basil
  1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and garlic
  3. cook and stir until mushrooms are a light golden brown and liquid has evaporated, about 10 minutes. Stir in basil.
  4. stir well
  5. serve
